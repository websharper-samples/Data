Tertiary school enrollment and GDP per capita in the EU vs the US

This workbook compares tertiary (college, university, etc.) school enrollment and GDP per capita (USD) figures in various European Union countries and the United States.

Data shown is obtained real-time from the World Bank database.

To add or refine these charts, you can edit the F# code in the primary tab.
